Our Mission: Make Fitness Accessible & Sustainable.

Why We Started

FitCycle was born from a simple idea: high-quality fitness gear is expensive, and fitness goals change. We saw too much gear end up in landfills while new enthusiasts struggled with high entry costs. Our solution is a circular economy for fitness—rent what you need, buy what you love, and give gear a second life.
